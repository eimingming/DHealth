'DHealth' is an introductory Alzheimer's disease diagnostic app. By showing the risk of disease as a percentage.

User can complete a quiz and the user's current life. The application analyzes user responses and displays the results. Users can see an initial introduction to Alzheimer's disease.

However, if users want to confirm 100%, they can see a doctor for further examination and treatment.
